Search Engine Optimization

SEO is vital to attracting customers to your website. When your site is easier to find, you rank higher in search results — and more people find you. E-commerce websites often have very large product catalogs with lots of web pages to optimize, complex duplicate content issues, and a lack of unique content across the entire site. This adds up to a need for an e-commerce SEO agency capable of identifying and optimizing these and other critical issues.
A few important elements in an e-commerce SEO campaign are:

  • Product categorization: This helps to properly prioritize pages for Google, so that search engines can distinguish more important from less important pages.

  • Keyword research: This is improtant to capturing traffic from not just a handful of high-volume strategic keywords, but also from the hundreds or thousands of related keyword terms whose combined volume is substantial.

  • On-site content production: Existing content at the product page level and category page level must be optimized and regularly updated, and additonal pages must be created to support the numerous keywords for which the site must be optimized.
